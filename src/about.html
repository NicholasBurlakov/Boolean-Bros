About Us

ParkSmart was founded 8 weeks ago with a mission to simplify parking through technology.

Inspiration

Inspired by the need for an easy and accessible way to pay for and reserve parking spots, ParkSmart was created by a group of passionate innovators. As students and emerging tech enthusiasts, we also embraced this project to gain invaluable experience in building web applications.

Core Values and Mission Statement

At ParkSmart, our core values revolve around innovation, accessibility, and reliability. We are committed to transforming parking management with smart technology solutions that ensure convenience for all users.

Mission Statement: "To innovate and streamline parking management, making it accessible and reliable for every vehicle owner."

Differentiation

Currently in development, ParkSmart plans to integrate advanced technology such as QR codes and AI-driven image recognition to monitor parking availability. This unique feature is under active development as we explore the most effective methods to serve our users better.

Achievements

A significant early success for us was developing a prototype system capable of detecting parked cars over QR codes, confirming spot availability in real-time.
